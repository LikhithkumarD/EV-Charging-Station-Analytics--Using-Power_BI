# 1. Project Overview

The **EV Charging Station Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed using Microsoft Power BI.

The project transforms raw operational data collected from EV charging stations into meaningful business insights through interactive reports, KPI monitoring, and data visualization.

The dashboard enables business users to monitor charging station performance, revenue generation, customer activity, charger utilization, and energy consumption from a centralized reporting platform.

---

# 2. Business Problem

Electric vehicle charging networks generate large volumes of operational data across multiple stations and cities.

Without a centralized reporting solution, organizations face challenges in:

* Monitoring station performance
* Tracking customer activity
* Measuring charging sessions
* Comparing city performance
* Monitoring revenue
* Evaluating charger utilization
* Supporting business decision-making

The objective of this project is to solve these challenges through an interactive Power BI dashboard.
